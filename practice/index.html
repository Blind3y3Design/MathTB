Keep Off the Grass!

Have you ever been tempted to take a shortcut across the grass? A student notices a worn diagonal path across the grass and considers taking the shortcut. Why do we call this a shortcut? Is it really shorter to cut across the lawn than to stay on the sidewalk? Let’s investigate.

 
Save Submit

In this Lesson...

You can figure out how much distance you’ll save by taking a shortcut, how high a kite is off the ground, or the dimensions of a flat-screen TV by using the Pythagorean theorem. Lets dig in.

Essential Questions

How are the side lengths of a right triangle related?

How can the Pythagorean theorem be applied to solve a variety of problems?

What You'll do

Inspect right triangles to search for possible patterns and relationships.

Use a variety of models to explain your discoveries.

Apply your discoveries to solve real-world problems involving right triangles.
